SEVERITY SCALE

HOW RESULTS ARE GRADED

Every scan resolves to one of three tiers, so the next step is never ambiguous.

CLEAR

Minor or cosmetic. Standard care advice is shown — no escalation needed.

MODERATE

Warrants monitoring and basic first aid. Logging the incident is recommended.

CRITICAL

Needs prompt attention — nearby hospital lookup and parent notification are surfaced immediately.
